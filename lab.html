Facilities/ Equipment Available:

  • State-of-the-art Infrastructure: Our CSE labs are equipped with modern and advanced infrastructure to facilitate cutting-edge research and practical learning.

Microcontroller & IoT Lab

Microcontroller & IoT Lab, located on the second floor, is a specialized facility at our college designed to cater to the growing demands of microcontroller programming and Internet of Things (IoT) development. This lab is equipped with 15 high-performance computers, ensuring students have access to powerful resources for programming microcontrollers and IoT applications. In addition to PCs, the lab also boasts 13 IoT devices, allowing students to work hands-on with real-world connected systems. This unique combination of hardware and software resources in the lab creates an ideal environment for students to explore and innovate in the exciting world of microcontrollers and IoT.
